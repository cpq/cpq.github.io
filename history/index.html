Human history condensed

Below is an attempt to reconstruct the flow of history of the humankind. Events are aligned in the chronological order, however the accurate positioning on the time scale for most of them is difficult.

1. Tribal society

  • barter - exchange with no profit
  • tribes have small communication radius, no distant travel
  • no money, no wars, no literacy

2. Invention of a profitable trade

  • trade with profit: the biggest invention of all
  • still barter, but TRADER exchanges with profit
  • trader travels, exchanges goods between distant tribes
  • indigenous tribals have total trust, profits are enourmous
  • forming of "trade houses" which proliferate fast
  • beginning of literacy and maths, for accounting

3. World expansion. Money. Capitalism. Marxist mindset

4. Weapons. Gangs. States. Feudalism. Religion

5. Moses escape. Bnei Mose. Libertarian mindset. Democracy

6. Bankers (catholics) vs Bnei Mose (protestants). War in Europe. Loss of democracy. Escape to USA

7. Bnei Mose last bastion lost. USA. Confederation. 1913 Fed

8. Crypto currency
